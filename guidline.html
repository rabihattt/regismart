Getting Started with Regismart

  1. Create Your Account: Click on the “Create Account” button and provide your email and a secure password to register.
  2. Log In: After registering, use the “Log In” button with your credentials to access your dashboard.
  3. Password Recovery: Forgot your password? Click on “Forget Password?” and follow the instructions to reset it securely.
  4. Keep Credentials Secure: Never share your password with anyone. Use a mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols for better security.
  5. Update Info: You can update your email or password anytime from your account settings after logging in.
  6. Contact Support: If you face any issues, reach out to our support team through the contact form on the website.
Note: For best experience, use Regismart on modern browsers like Chrome, Firefox, or Edge. Keep your browser updated for security and performance.
